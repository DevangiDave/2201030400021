Plant Care Tips

Here are some of the best tips from our members on how to keep your plants healthy and happy.

  • Water your plants according to their needs. Some plants like to be moist, while others prefer to dry out between waterings. Check the soil with your finger before watering.
  • Provide enough light for your plants. Some plants need bright, direct sunlight, while others thrive in low, indirect light. Find out what your plants prefer and place them accordingly.
  • Prune your plants regularly. Remove any dead, diseased, or damaged leaves and stems to keep your plants looking neat and prevent infections. You can also trim your plants to shape them or encourage new growth.
  • Repot your plants when they outgrow their pots. Choose a pot that is slightly larger than the current one and has drainage holes. Use fresh potting soil and gently loosen the roots before planting.
  • Fertilize your plants occasionally. Most plants benefit from a balanced fertilizer every few months during the growing season. Follow the instructions on the label and avoid over-fertilizing.
